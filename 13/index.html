Delta Airlines: A Major Airline of the United States


About Delta Air Lines

Delta Airlines commonly known as Delta is a major American carrier with its headquarters in Atlanta, Georgia. Although it has nine hubs namely Boston, Los Angeles, New York-JFK, New York-LaGuardia, Detroit, Salt Lake City, Minneapolis-Saint Paul and Seattle, Atlanta is the main hub for Delta. The airline is ranked among the largest airlines in the world in terms of fleet size, operation and number of passengers. It has massive operations with over 5,000 flights daily connecting more than 300 destinations in six continents. Delta Air is also the founding member of the airline alliance, SkyTeam. The airline also has joint ventures with the following airlines: Virgin Atlantic, Virgin Australia, Korean Air, Aeromexico, LATAM and Air France. Delta has to its fleet over 900 aircraft comprising McDonnell Douglas Airbus and Boeing. The frequent flyer program of Delta is called SkyMiles.

Delta Air in-flight services

There are six cabins of travel aboard Delta flights being First Class, Delta One, Delta Comfort, Delta Premium Select, Main cabin and Basic Economy. Here’s a glimpse at what each Delta cabin offers:

  • First Class : A personalised service starting with a pre-flight drink along with snacks, built-in 110-volt outlets so that you stay connected and entertained. Every first class passenger is also provided with Earbuds by Billboard and coffee and snacks by Starbucks. Full-service meals are served on flights over 1,500 miles.
  • Delta One : The cabin can be experienced on long-haul international and select domestic circuits. It offers a personal flight attendant tending to all your needs, 180 degree flat-bed seat, Delta Studio for entertainment, TUMI amenity kit consisting of Le Labo lotion, eyeshades, a pair of socks, lip balm and other essentials.
  • Delta Premium Select : This superior cabin is available on select international flights. A wider seat with a better recline and an elevated footrest, a bigger entertainment screen available at seat or on a device, power ports located at the front, plated meals served in Alessi service ware paired with the finest wines.
  • Main Cabin : Enjoy the unlimited entertainment powered by Delta Studio on the seatback with complimentary headphones. Medallion members can pre-select the seat of their choice at no extra charge. Free snacks on flights over 250 miles including Biscoff cookies, a selection of Coca Cola or Starbucks products to a wide range of alcoholic drinks available for purchase.
  • Delta Comfort : The seats have a dedicated overhead bin space, free amenity kit, a wider legroom, early boarding so that you can use the designated overhead luggage space. Munch on premium snacks on flights over 900 miles and Starbucks tidbits on flights over 300 miles. Upgrades available for Medallion members.
  • Basic Economy : Travel in a main cabin seat and enjoy complimentary snacks and soft beverages. Purchase WiFi on select flights and avail free of charge access to in-flight messaging along with entertainment by Delta Studio and a permit to carry a bag in the cabin for free.

Delta Airlines Online Booking

To make a Delta Air online booking visit the official website and select ‘book.’ Enter the departure and arrival airports, choose the type of trip from one-way, round and multi-city, date of departure and number of passengers. The search can be filtered with options such as refundable fares which reimburses some amount in the event of cancellation, ‘shop with miles’ which lets you book your flight with the miles earned or ‘my dates are flexible’ giving you the best fares.

Why Do People Call Delta Airlines Phone Number?

People call Delta customer support for a range of reasons, including:

  • Booking, making changes to or canceling flight reservations
  • Flight rebooking
  • Baggage claim issues, including lost and damaged baggage queries
  • Questions about flight policies, such as the cost of checked baggage
  • Making arrangements for people with special needs, children traveling alone, or traveling with pets
  • Questions about SkyMiles, Delta's frequent-flyer program
  • Questions about flight times, schedules and delays
  • Billing issues

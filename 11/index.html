United Airlines: a major airline in the United States
United Airlines is a major American airline that has its headquarters in Chicago. United has nine hubs including Boston, Los Angeles, New York-JFK, New York-LaGuardia, Detroit, Salt Lake City, Minneapolis-Saint Paul and Seattle. Even with all of those headquarters, Atlanta is United's main hub on the planet. United Air is the world's largest airline. It has over 4,500 flights per day and more than 250 destinations in six different continents. The airline also has joint ventures with Virgin Atlantic, Virgin Australia, Korean Air, Aero Mexico, LATAM and Air France. United has to its fleet over 900 aircraft including McDonnell Dougal.

United Air Lines baggage allowance
Everyone has their own luggage restrictions, depending on which ticket they're flying on. Economy passengers get 23 kg of baggage, while United Comfort arrives can only carry 32 kg. First class passengers are allowed to have 1 carry-on bag and one personal item with them per flight - regardless of what kind of ticket is being used.

United's popular routes are their regular routes
Some popular routes between cities at United Airlines include New York to Boston, Atlanta to Houston, Seattle to Miami, Washington to New York, Chicago to New York, and San Jose to New York. There are also popular international routes like those between New York and London and those between Los Angeles and Paris.

United Air offer a range of in-flight services: -
United provides travelers with different cabin options. Each cabin has its own perks: -
- First Class - Full-service meals are served on any flight over 1500 miles. First Class guests are provided with a pre-flight drink and snacks, an Earbud headphone and his or her choice of coffee from Starbucks.
- United One - This is a cabin specially designed for United Airlines. It's available on select long-haul international flights and in-flight entertainment comes from the impressive United Studio, where you can be entertained by some great shows.
- United Premium Select - This cabin on select international flights is one of a kind. It has an elevated footrest and a bigger entertainment screen available at your seat, power ports are located in front, the meals served can be paired with fine wines.
- Main Cabin - Free snacks, premium entertainment and more can be found on United Studio's complimentary headphones. We make sure our passengers are getting the most out of their flying experience with Medallion members.
- United Comfort - Overhead bins for with more space for luggage, complimentary amenity kits and legrooms that allow early access. Premium snacks on flights over 900 miles, Starbucks tidbits on flights over 300 miles. The reward is the Medallion Card when you fly the airline with us.
- Basic Economy - Check in early so you can enjoy complimentary amenities such as snacks and drinks. Enjoy WiFi on selected flights and stay connected during your journey with access to entertainment from United Studio and a free bag allowance.


United Airlines Frequently Asked Questions
What is the policy for cancelling a United Air flight booking?
Depending on the fare rules.

What are the policies for cancellations or refunds for flying United Air?
If you purchased a non-refundable ticket, there is a cancellation fee that may be deducted from the original cost of your ticket. Beyond that, any remaining value will be provided as an eCredit that can be used in purchasing another ticket (eCredit is valid for one year from the date the ticket was issued).

Do United Airlines' mobile apps allow tracking my checked baggage?
Yes, United Airlines makes it easy to track your baggage status on their smartphone app.

Can I make an online check-in for United flights?
You can check in online up to 24 hours before the scheduled departure of your flight.

Can I buy an extra seat if I'm carrying a fragile item on a United flight?
If traveling with something you don’t want to check-in or put in the overhead bin of the aircraft, we offer purchases that you can do in advance. You must meet some requirements for your items -The package should weigh less than 45 kg, should not restrict access to or use of required emergency exits, will not hide passengers from the flight’s belt, should not be smoking in that seat, and will prevent any potential injuries due to shifting bags during flight.

How many United Airlines flights are operated daily?
United Airlines operate flights daily on 5,200 of their flights.

Does United Airlines offer online check-in?
Yes, United Airlines has a web check-in process.
